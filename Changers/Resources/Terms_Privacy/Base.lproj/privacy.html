3. Online Session Information (Cookies and Web Beacons)

3.1. The Changers Website uses so-called “Cookies” to recollect repeated uses of our services by the same User / subscriber. Cookies“ are alphanumeric identifiers in the form of text files that are inserted and stored by your Web browser on your computer's hard drive. For example, Changers may assign a cookie to you to help better determine which Changers offer to serve to you.

3.2. Most of the Cookies we use are so-called “Session-Cookies” which are deleted after you close your browser. 3.3. Please note that most Internet browsers will allow you to stop cookies from being stored on your computer and to delete cookies stored on your computer. If you choose to eliminate cookies, the full functionality of the Service may be impaired for you.

5.1. The Operator wants to ensure the User's complete control over their personal data. This provision applies without prejudice to the User's legal claim for information (see Art. VII of this PP). The deletion of the Users personal mobility-data does not include the aggregated and anonymized data the Operator gathers to create statistics of collective energy-saving-efforts of the Changers Community.

5.5. You can send further questions regarding the collection of mobility-data via the Changers App to the Provider's customer service at any time under the e-Mail-address given in the Website's legal notice.
